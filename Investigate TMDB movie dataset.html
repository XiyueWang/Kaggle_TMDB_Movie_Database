Introduction¶

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue. This project aim to explore 1) which movie genres are popular year to year? 2) Which properties are associated with high revenue? 3) Which director can bring high revenues in general.

Conclusions¶

  • Before 2000, people have diverse interest in movie genres, usally most popular genres switched year to year. After 2000, people like Fantasy and Adventure movies, beside 2011 and 2012, in which western movies took the lead.
  • Base on the dataset, popularity, vote counts and budges are positively correlates with the revenue.
  • From the dataset, Clyde Geronimi brings highest revenue.

Limitations

  • In the analysis, all NaN values are dropped. They may be replaced by other value, e.g. mean or caclulate from regression.
  • There are different ways to inteperate the conclusions. For example, high vote count may result from a high-revenue movie (more people watched), so it might not be a good predictor for high revenue.
  • All the conclusions drew without machine learning process.
